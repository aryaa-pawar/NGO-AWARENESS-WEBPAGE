Who We Are

Driven by Compassion, Committed to Change

InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization founded on 23 September 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla. The organization operates across India with a mission to create sustainable social impact through community-driven initiatives.

The foundation focuses on education, environmental conservation, women empowerment, animal welfare, hunger relief, and youth skill development.

Our Impact Philosophy

Why Our Work Matters

Every project is designed to address systemic challenges, creating a self-sustaining cycle of social growth and ecological recovery.

Education

Helping children gain access to learning opportunities, digital literacy, and basic academic mentorship, breaking the generational cycle of poverty.

Sustainability

Promoting environmental conservation through organized plantation drives, green field cleanups, and educating local communities on eco-friendly living habits.

Empowerment

Supporting underprivileged women, marginalized youths, and struggling communities through skill-building, career guidance, and hygiene support.
